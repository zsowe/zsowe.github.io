2024

navu atta -

debut LP




more information
navu atta, a full length release & visual project
made over the course of 2 years.


the name is derived from native american and buddhist concepts of
astral projection, depersonalisation, and out-of-body experiences.


musically, the album straddles a line between potent dissociative
and cathartic emotional release - detailed synth
flourishes, almost familiar melodies, disintegrated textures
found sound and vinyl artifacts.


the approach to the film - a collage of hundreds
of stolen moments collected over years, manipulated or distorted
in post often to unrecognisable effect,
electrical light, celestial bodies, vast natural
+ liminal human spaces, and the relative absence of human subjects.


the subject of the film is a mute observer, evoking the titular
shoshone concept of navuesheip or "wandering soul",
which is free to leave the body during dreams or trancelike states.


in the mountains an elegant woman dressed in all red,
twirling a parasol, poses for photos in front
of the tower at huang mua peak.
observed from a distance, at 300 feet above ground,
she is cast against a backdrop of enormous emptiness.


there is no particualr mission statement
or manifesto relating to this project -
our priority is simply the aesthetics that appeal to us as artists.
we only wish to present a work that people can
draw their own interpretation from.
navu atta has been described back to us in opposites,
as both meditative and dissociative, comforting and challenging.

2022

denizen forest -

debut EP




more information
denizen forest is my first, mostly solo effort.
a project done in order to start releasing as 'zsowe'-
which meant as a collaborative project, led by myself,
often features people and artists from my life as
collaborators, helping to realise the vision.

the project is a collection of 5 tracks that differ genre
as well as stylistically, whilst keeping the energy
and atmosphere in the same playing field, transitioning each
track into the next, creating a seamless listening experience

visually, the short films for denizen forest are first
attempts at collaging my footage to the music in order to
include the visual medium into the zsowe project.

denizen forest showcases the beginnings of the style
and direction of the work that is to come in the future.
