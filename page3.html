2024

navu atta -

debut LP




more information
navu atta, an ambient full length release & full-length visual effort
made over the course of 2 years.
the name is derived from native american and buddhist concepts of
astral projection, depersonalisation, and out-of-body experiences
musically, the album straddles the line between potent dissociative
and cathartic emotional release as myriads of detailed synth
flourishes and almost familiar melodies give way to cavernous
disintegrated textures, found sound and vinyl artifacts.


the approach to the film is much the same; a collage of hundreds
of stolen moments collected over years and manipulated or distorted
in post often to unrecognisable effect,
motifs do arise - electrical light, celestial bodies, vast natural,
liminal human spaces, and the relative absence of human subjects.

the subject of the film is a mute observer, evoking the titular
shoshone concept of navuesheip or "wandering soul",
which is free to leave the body during dreams or trancelike states.


technically, this album and film were made through a daily process of
recording and editing most comparable to collage.
daily recording sessions primarily used the deepmind 12,
ibanez electric bass and a yamaha electric keyboard,
played to Alice Coltrane, Sun Ra, and Azymuth records.
these long improvisational sessions would then be
ruthlessly manipulated, deconstructed and re-arranged in fl-studio.

Tim Hecker fans should feel quite at home with the use of progressive
motifs played over disintegrated rhythmic structures,
and free/spirital jazz appreciators may recognise influences in the
occasional degraded sample or echoed phrase.


visually the material is more grounded, frequently involving ordinary
locations, while taking us through a variety of settings -
rock formations, forests, and architecture all take on a haunting
quality when emptied of their people, who are all either just
out of frame or faceless and distant.
the title sequence perfectly summarises this alienation -

in the mountains an elegant woman dressed in all red,
twirling a parasol, poses for photos in front
of the tower at huang mua peak.
observed from a distance, at 300 feet above ground,
she is cast against a backdrop of enormous emptiness.


there is no particualr mission statement
or manifesto relating to this project -
our priority is simply the aesthetics that appeal to us as artists.
we only wish to present a work that people can
draw their own interpretation from.
navu atta has been described back to us in opposites,
as both meditative and dissociative, comforting and challenging.

the above is merely intended as a frame of reference
as to how the project was made and an insight into the approach.

2022

denizen forest -

debut EP




more information
denizen forest is my first, mostly solo effort.
a project done in order to start releasing as 'zsowe'-
which meant as a collaborative project, led by myself,
often features people and artists from my life as
collaborators, helping to realise the vision.

the project is a collection of 5 tracks that differ genre wise,
as well as stylistically, whilst keeping the energy and atmosphere in
the same playing field, transitioning each track into the next,
creating a seamless listening experience.

visually, the short films for denizen forest are first attempts at
collaging my footage to the music in order to
include the visual medium into the zsowe project.

denizen forest showcases the beginnings of the style and direction
of the work that is to come in the future.
